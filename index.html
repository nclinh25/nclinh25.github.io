Project 1: Operational Reporting & Workflow Automation Tool

Excel | VBA | Dashboard Reporting | Workflow Automation | Process Improvement

Business Problem

The business needed a reliable daily source of truth for operational activity, customer volume, product movement, chargeable services, and weekly performance. Before this tool, key activities were captured through manual processes, paper records, whiteboards, and disconnected communication.

This made it difficult for teams to understand what had been received, loaded out, moved, charged, or missed during the week. Management often had to wait until the following Monday to understand the previous week’s activity, which limited the ability to respond early to volume changes, labour demand, or billing issues.

My Solution

I designed and built an Excel-based operational reporting and tracking tool from scratch within two weeks. The workbook captures daily receivable activity, load-out activity, customer volume, service type, transport usage, destination trends, and performance indicators.

I chose Excel because users needed one practical workbook where they could review dashboard summaries and drill down into order-level details when required. I built calculation logic, dashboard views, and VBA-supported refresh processes to improve usability and reduce workbook slowdown.

Key Features

  • Receivable tracking for inbound orders, service type, quantity, variance, temperature, and transport details.
  • Load-out tracking for booking, customer, order, product, container, destination, transport, services, and documentation requirements.
  • Daily and weekly dashboard views showing cartons, orders, customer volume, service activity, and week-on-week performance.
  • Calculation sheet and refresh button to reduce continuous formula recalculation and improve workbook performance.
  • Structured source-of-truth workbook used by operations, administration, finance, and management.

Business Impact

2 weeks Built from scratch
10+ Daily users
90% Manual effort reduction
$4K+ Labour cost reduction

The tool reduced manual tracking and checking effort by approximately 90%, improved daily visibility, and supported more than $4,000 in agency labour cost reduction within one month by helping management better understand daily workload and operational demand.

Project 2: Invoice Reconciliation & Revenue Protection Process

Power Query | Excel | PDF Import | Data Validation | Variance Analysis

Business Problem

The business faced a major billing accuracy issue because the connection between the source system and invoice system was no longer fully reliable. Invoices were previously assumed to be correct, but mischarges, overcharges, missing charges, and duplicated charges were later identified.

These invoice issues created customer dissatisfaction, delayed payments, and business risk. The previous checking process required manual line-by-line review and could take one to two days across more than 20 customer invoices each week.

My Solution

I created a weekly invoice reconciliation process using Excel and Power Query. The tool compares expected charges generated from source system activity against invoice PDFs generated for customers.

The reconciliation is rules-based because different customers have different contract terms, service rules, carton-based charges, pallet-based charges, and customer-specific billing logic. The tool identifies missing invoice lines, mischarged services, overcharges, and quantity differences.

Weekly Process

  • Export weekly source data, including operational scanner activity and manual billing tasks.
  • Save source data and invoice PDFs into weekly folders for audit evidence.
  • Update the Power Query source to the weekly invoice PDF folder.
  • Refresh the template to generate variance summary and detailed variance breakdown.
  • Review key variances and send confirmed findings to accounting for amendment.
  • Accounting waits for confirmation before finalising and issuing invoices.

Business Impact

20+ Invoices checked weekly
30 min New checking time
$175K+ Missing charges identified
~$200K Delayed payments supported

The process reduced invoice checking from one to two days to approximately 30 minutes including review. It identified more than $175,000 in missing charges and supported the recovery of approximately $200,000 in delayed customer payments by providing clear reconciliation evidence.

Project 3: Digital Invoice Form & Rate Governance Tool

Excel Form | VBA | Rate Matrix | PDF Automation | Digital Transformation

Business Problem

The business used a handwritten yellow docket process to capture chargeable services, product details, and order information. Over time, more than 10 physical log books were accumulated. The process was slow, paper-based, and dependent on physical handover, scanning, and clear handwriting.

The Rate Matrix also needed stronger control. It was meant to be the source of truth for customer pricing, but too many people could edit it, creating risks of accidental changes, incorrect rate updates, and inconsistency between customer contracts, system rates, and invoice charges.

My Solution

I built an Excel-based digital invoice form to replace the handwritten docket process. The form captures order number, customer, date, invoice number, product details, and chargeable warehouse services such as labelling, shrink wrapping, port marks, and other customer-specific services.

I also rebuilt the Rate Matrix into a controlled pricing tool. Users can select a customer, load customer accounts, load relevant services, view the latest rate, and update new prices through a controlled form. The underlying customer pricing sheets are locked and password-protected to reduce accidental edits.

Key Features

  • Excel-based digital invoice form for warehouse users.
  • Save Product, Issue Invoices, and Save as PDF buttons.
  • Automatic PDF saving to a shared drive, removing the need to scan handwritten forms.
  • Customer and customer account selection process.
  • Load Services button showing the latest service rates.
  • Update New Price button that records new prices while keeping historical rate records.
  • Protected Rate Matrix sheets to improve pricing governance.

Business Impact

10+ Log books replaced
20+ Customers supported
30 Service lines supported
2 → 1 Admin effort reduced

The digital form reduced reliance on handwritten paperwork, improved charge readability, reduced scanning and printer dependency, and improved pricing control. The process is currently used alongside the yellow docket process during transition, allowing users to confirm accuracy before fully moving away from paper.
